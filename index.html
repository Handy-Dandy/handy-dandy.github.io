About this Site

The purpose of this site is to make it easier to communicate with your local officials while keeping things template-free!

Email filters can easily get rid of template emails. The impact is so much greater with unique emails!

Use some of the forms below to generate a mailto link. Then when you click the link it will, open in your email app.

This makes sending the email easier, while still making it your own!
